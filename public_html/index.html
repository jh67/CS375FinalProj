About The Clan

The Champions of Leisure is a universal clan focusing on teamwork, character development and socializing. Unity, kindness, and teamwork is our motto and we intend to abide by it. We have a few rules to keep our clan's order and maintain the peace. They can all be summed up as, "Treat others as how you would like to be treated."
